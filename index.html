About Us

Welcome to Daya Hospital, where healthcare meets compassion, and every patient journey is our shared responsibility.

At Daya Hospital, we extend beyond the realm of medical care. We embrace our role as social workers, deeply committed to fostering positive change in our community.

Our journey is woven with a tapestry of care, empathy, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. We don't just treat ailments; we empower lives and stand as beacons of hope.

Driven by the belief that healthcare extends beyond hospital walls, we actively engage in initiatives that uplift and empower our community. From supporting women's health to contributing to social causes, Daya Hospital is more than a healthcare institution; we are a pillar of social responsibility.

Come join hands with us as we continue this remarkable journey, Every Step of the Way.

Community Outreach

At Daya Hospital, our commitment extends beyond the confines of medical care. Together with our dedicated team, we actively engage in transformative social work, fostering health and well-being in our community. Our collective efforts resonate in initiatives aimed at providing essential healthcare services, promoting awareness, and contributing to the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. Through our voluntary endeavors, we channel our energies into creating a healthier and cleaner environment, reflecting our unwavering dedication to the welfare of society. Every step we take in these initiatives is a testament to our belief that healthcare goes hand in hand with compassion and community service..

In a heartwarming chapter of our history, Daya Hospital initiated the "Ladoo Ayi, Khushiya Layi" scheme, a celebration of the joy that newborns bring into families. Recognizing the pivotal role of mothers in shaping the future, we embarked on a mission to honor and support them. Through this scheme, we extended cash prizes to the incredible women who welcomed these precious little angels into the world. The initiative not only celebrated the miracle of life but also aimed to alleviate the financial burden on families during the early days of parenthood. "Ladoo Ayi, Khushiya Layi" stood as a testament to our belief in spreading happiness and contributing to the well-being of both mothers and their newborns. It reflected our commitment to not only provide medical care but to embrace the broader aspects of joy and support in the journey of parenthood..
